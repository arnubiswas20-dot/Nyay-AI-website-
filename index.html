Gender inequality creates the culture. But evidence tampering gives criminals the escape route. Nyay AI seals that escape route — forever.

Pen & Paper Evidence Logs

Officers still document crime scenes on paper — editable, losable, destroyable. A bribe buys a rewrite. The first version of truth disappears.

→ No original photo timestamp. No tamper detection.

Lab Handoffs With No Lock

Physical evidence reaches the lab with zero cryptographic verification. Lab technicians can alter reports, swap samples — no audit trail exists.

→ DNA reports changed. Cases dismissed. Accused walks free.

→ Justice delayed becomes justice denied.

// How The Lock Works

Every Byte
Has A Witness.

The moment evidence is registered, our system creates a SHA-256 cryptographic hash — a unique fingerprint of that data. Change even one pixel of an image, one letter of a report — the hash changes. The lie becomes visible.

This hash is stored on an immutable blockchain ledger. No administrator, no police officer, no corrupt official can alter what's been logged. The truth is permanent.
